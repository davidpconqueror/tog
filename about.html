About us

We are a team of missionaries committed to glorifying God by preaching the Good News, training young men and women, helping the needy, and planting churches across India.

Tabernacle of Grace Ministries is an evangelical, indigenous, and holistic faith-based missionary organization. We strive to reach the unreached, bring revival among churches, and raise up the next generation of mission workers by sharing the love of God — both in word and deed.

Our ministry is officially registered with the Government of Punjab, India, and is actively involved in a wide range of outreach and training efforts.

We hope this site encourages you to pray for each of our ministries. We would be honored to have you join us in the work God is doing through Tabernacle of Grace Ministries.

May the Lord bless you — and please remember TABERNACLE OF GRACE MINISTRIES in your prayers.
